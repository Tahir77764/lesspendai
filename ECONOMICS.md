# ECONOMICS.md - Unit Economics & Monetization

## 1. Core Value Proposition
Less Spend AI delivers immediate, quantifiable ROI. The product eliminates "Shadow AI Spend", bypasses minimum seat traps, and identifies functional redundancies (e.g., paying for both Copilot and Cursor). For every minute invested in the audit, users discover clear mathematical and feature-matched savings on their AI software stack.

## 2. Revenue Model (Monetization Paths)
While the current MVP serves as a highly effective lead-generation magnet, the long-term economics rely on three distinct paths:

1. **High-Ticket Consulting Upsells (Active):**
   The platform automatically detects enterprise-level savings (>$500/month). For these users, the system natively pitches a "Consultation with Credex" to actively implement the savings and manage their tech stack.
2. **Affiliate & Partner Revenue (Planned):**
   When the hybrid Gemini optimization engine recommends a tool swap (e.g., ChatGPT -> Claude Pro), the generated links can include affiliate tracking, earning a commission on the converted user.
3. **B2B Continuous Monitoring (Future):**
   A premium subscription model for IT managers and CFOs. Instead of a one-time audit, the platform connects to corporate expense cards/SSO to continually auto-flag unapproved AI SaaS sprawl in real-time.

## 3. Unit Economics
- **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS):** Extremely low and highly scalable.
  - **Hosting:** Next.js on Vercel/Netlify.
  - **AI Processing:** Gemini 1.5 Flash API is utilized efficiently (only firing on comparable alternatives), costing fractions of a cent per audit.
  - **Email Delivery:** Nodemailer via standard SMTP.
  - **Database:** Supabase (Postgres) handling lightweight lead storage.
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Near zero initially. Growth relies on organic SEO and the Product-Led Growth (PLG) shareable link loop.
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Extremely high for enterprise leads funneled into Credex consulting services.
